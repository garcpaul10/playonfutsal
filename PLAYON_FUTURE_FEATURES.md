# PlayOn — Future Features Backlog

This living document tracks features planned for future phases. Items are grouped by phase and domain.

## Phase 2 — Scheduling & Game Management
- Game scheduling engine: assign courts, time slots, and teams to game slots within a league season
- Score entry for completed games (admin/referee)
- Standings calculation (points, GD, W/L/D)
- Game result history per team
- Referee assignment to scheduled games
- Automated email/SMS notification when schedule is published

## Phase 3 — Payments & Payouts
- Stripe integration for player/team registration payments
- Payment plans and installment support
- Refund processing workflow
- Referee and coach payout management (batch payroll export)
- Financial reporting dashboard (revenue by program, period)
- Invoice generation for teams/organizations

## Phase 4 — Team & Roster Management
- Team roster management (add/remove players)
- Player eligibility checks by age group
- Roster lock date enforcement
- Jersey number assignment
- Parent/Guardian linkage to player profiles (for minors)
- Waiver/consent form collection per registration

## Phase 5 — Brackets & Tournament Progression
- Bracket generation (single elimination, double elimination, round robin)
- Live bracket visualization
- Match result entry and bracket advancement
- Seeding by ranking or manual input
- Third-place game support

## Phase 6 — Player QR Codes & Check-in
- Durable QR code generated at player profile creation (PlayOn ID)
- QR code scanner for drop-in check-in (mobile-optimized)
- Attendance tracking per drop-in session
- Digital player ID card view

## Phase 7 — Referee & Coach Management
- Referee profile creation (certification level, availability)
- Coach profile creation (bio, certifications)
- Assignment workflow: staff assigns ref/coach to event
- Availability calendar per referee/coach
- Payout rate configuration per role/event type

## Phase 8 — Communications & Notifications
- In-app notification center
- Email digest: upcoming sessions, schedule changes, payment reminders
- SMS alerts for cancellations and reschedules (Twilio)
- Bulk announcements from admin to program participants

## Phase 9 — Analytics & Reporting
- Registration trends over time
- Revenue analytics by program type, age group, season
- Court utilization heatmaps
- Player retention metrics (returning vs new registrants)
- Export reports to CSV/PDF

## Phase 10 — Mobile App (Expo)
- Native iOS/Android app via Expo
- Push notifications for schedule updates and game reminders
- QR code scanner (native camera access)
- Offline-friendly schedule view
- Deep links to program registration

## Phase 11 — Advanced League Features
- Free agent pool (players without a team can be discovered by teams)
- Trade/transfer window between teams
- Relegation/promotion between divisions
- Season-over-season player statistics tracking
- Head-to-head history

## Phase 12 — Venue & Court Management
- Court maintenance scheduling (mark unavailable)
- Multi-venue support (additional locations)
- Court conflict detection when scheduling games
- Facility fee management

## Phase 13 — Waiver & Compliance
- Digital waiver collection and e-signature (DocuSign or native)
- Age verification for adult programs
- Medical information collection (allergies, conditions)
- GDPR/COPPA compliance for minors

## Phase 14 — Sponsorship & Marketing
- Sponsor logo display on public pages
- Sponsored program branding
- Promo code / discount code support for registrations
- Referral program (earn credit for referring new players)

## Phase 15 — API & Integrations
- Public-facing API for third-party integrations
- Google Calendar sync for player schedules
- Zapier webhook support
- Game data export for external stats platforms
